c/u/Slot Machine Screen

u/Stake
Set the amount of money you wish to bet in the Stake drop-down menu.

u/Play
Use the Play button to spin the reels. 

u/Nudge
Nudges are awarded for double fruits. When nudges are available you can nudge the slot reels down one position. You cannot change the stake amount when you have nudges.

c/u/Rules

The aim of the game is to match 3 fruits. The prize returned will depend on the fruits you match.

Oranges = Stake x 5
Plums = Stake x 10
Bananas = Stake x 15
Apples = Stake x 20
Grapes = Stake x 25s
Cherries = Stake x 30
Pineapples = Stake x 40
Strawberries = Stake x 50
